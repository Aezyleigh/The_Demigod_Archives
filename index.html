Message from Chiron

Greetings, young demigods and curious mortals. I am Chiron, activities director of Camp Half-Blood. This archive has been compiled to help both new and experienced half-bloods understand their heritage, the dangers they face, and the incredible world of gods and monsters that exists just beyond mortal sight.

Within these archives, you will find information about the cabins, your potential godly parents, the brave heroes who have walked these grounds before you, and the epic quests that have shaped our world. Study well, for knowledge is as important a weapon as celestial bronze when facing the challenges ahead.

Yours in training,
Chiron
